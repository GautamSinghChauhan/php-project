Mandatory provisions of Right To Information Act 2005 Ref. Clause b (i) to (xvii) under Section 4, Sub-section 1

NIXI has been declared a public authority under Section 2 (h) of RTI Act 2005. Accordingly under Section 4 (b) of the act it is required to publish the particulars of its organization, functions and duties etc and update these on a regular basis. In compliance with the provisions of RTI Act 2005, NIXI furnishes the details below.

Related information to RTI 2005

Right to Information Act 2005

Procedure relating to seeking information from NIXI under the RTI Act 2005: Any person seeking information from NIXI should submit the application  under Section 6 of the RTI Act, 2005 to the PIO, NIXI

The applicants may send their application form to the PIO mentioned above. The fee charged for supplying of information by NIXI will be in accordance with provisions of Right to Information Act 2005.

Right of the citizen in case of denial of information: Incase of denial of the information, a citizen may appeal to the Appellate authority mentioned above as per the procedure set-out in RTI Act 2005.
